# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to create a welcoming and respectful environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience.

## Our Standards
We expect community members to act with kindness, professionalism, and integrity. Harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards and taking fair, consistent action when rules are violated.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including online discussions, project repositories, and official events.

## Enforcement
Instances of misconduct can be reported to project maintainers. Reports will be handled promptly, confidentially, and fairly.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Consequences for violations may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal, depending on the severity and frequency of the behavior.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1, a widely recognized standard for open-source communities.
